Our library

If it wasn't enough to have: 10 accommodation rooms, a restaurant, a swimming pool, a swing and a cat, now we have the "Success Library". It is not just a simple activity room, but rather a space for personal development. Development can be on any level, in any field, depending on what you want.

To inspire our guests, we have placed shelves with the most valuable books, we believe, for personal and professional development. We invite you at any time to borrow any title that catches your eye, with nothing more than the responsibility to return it in the same condition as before.

Inside you will find:

  • Air condition
  • Video projector + projection screen
  • Flipchart
  • Audio system
  • Wireless internet
