Our restaurant

A location just a few steps from the REGIE Campus, where the Moldoveanu family, your host, wants you to feel good and have, besides a tasty meal, a very pleasant evening.

Our small restaurant is specially designed to create an intimate atmosphere in which to enjoy a special evening in the company of loved ones. Even the chairs are a model designed by us with the express requirement to be comfortable! A little pampering doesn't hurt ...The specific is Romanian, but don't be surprised to find tasty dishes from international cuisine. A small terrace under the lap of two fir trees offers you a pleasant setting for both a morning coffee and a beer in fact in the evening. We are open for cultural events, mini-exhibitions, mini-concerts….

In our small restaurant you can enjoy a quiet dinner with friends, business partners or you can choose to invite them to a barbecue in the yard prepared by you or us…. We are glad that since 2003 until now we have stepped on the threshold of guests from 27 nations, from four continents.
